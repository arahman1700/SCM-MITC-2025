Committee Scope & Objectives

Physical Verification

Conducting thorough count, technical classification, and financial evaluation of all materials and assets across all NIT warehouses and project sites.

Audit Point Closure

Systematically addressing and closing all outstanding findings from previous internal audits to ensure compliance and process improvement.

Asset Optimization

Strategically identifying usable materials for internal reallocation to projects, minimizing procurement costs and maximizing resource utilization.

Scrap Management

Classifying and appraising all non-usable materials designated as scrap for profitable disposal and storage space recovery.
